About me

I’m a Senior Software Engineer at Microsoft focused on AI-first systems, security, and compliance. I build secure-by-default platforms—policy-as-code guardrails, data protection, model/prompt risk controls, and telemetry for real-time detection—so product teams can ship AI quickly while staying audit-ready.

My research spans Ethical AI and XAI with publications in IEEE and Springer venues. I also serve as a reviewer, judge, and speaker across the AI ecosystem. Outside work, I’m a photographer who loves telling stories through light, texture, and stillness.
