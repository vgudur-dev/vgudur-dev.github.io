About

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Microsoft with over 7 years of experience designing and delivering secure, scalable web applications across the entire technology stack. Currently, I architect and implement solutions for Microsoft Teams that safeguard 145M+ global users.

My expertise spans frontend frameworks like React and Angular, backend services with GraphQL and REST APIs, and cloud platforms including AWS and Azure. I'm passionate about building intuitive user interfaces, leading cross-functional teams, and mentoring the next generation of engineers.

As an active researcher and AI Frontier Ambassador, I contribute to the academic community through peer-reviewed publications, book chapters in leading volumes from Springer Nature, Wiley, and IGI Global, and extensive academic service including program committee memberships for AIES, BDAA, and ICAIC, advisory committee roles, and publication committee chair positions. I also serve as a judge for prestigious competitions including Globee Awards, university showcases, and cutting-edge hackathons. My research spans AI-driven software engineering, autonomous cybersecurity systems, and ethical AI frameworks with practical applications in enterprise environments.
